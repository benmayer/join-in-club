Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01. Jul 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Join in, ("Join in", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares personal data when you use the Join in app (the "Service"). We are the data controller for this processing.

If you have questions about this policy or want to exercise a right described below, contact us at hello@joinin.club

1. Data We Collect

Category What it includes Source
Profile data Name, photo, sport preferences, connections You, at signup
Session data Sessions you create, join, or request to join; messages within a session You, during use
Location data Device GPS location expo-location, when the app is in use
Photos Camera roll / camera access for profile and session images expo-image-picker, when you choose to upload
Usage data App interactions, events, email address (used to identify you across sessions) PostHog, automatically
Crash and diagnostic data Crash reports, device model, OS version Sentry and expo-device, automatically
Push token Device token used to deliver notifications expo-notifications, if you enable notifications
Email (transactional) Address used to send report confirmations and account emails Resend, when triggered by your actions

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate core features: creating, browsing, and joining sessions, and messaging other users
  • To show you nearby sessions on the map (Mapbox) based on your location
  • To send push notifications about session activity, invitations, and join requests
  • To send transactional emails, for example report confirmations
  • To understand app usage and improve the product (PostHog)
  • To diagnose crashes and bugs (Sentry)

3. Location Data

We use foreground location (only while the app is open) to show you nearby sessions and to power the map view. Location is not collected in the background. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time, though this will limit or disable location-dependent features.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with advertisers.

5. International Data Transfers

Some of our processors may store or process data outside Germany and the EU. Where this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (including the GDPR if you are in the EEA or UK), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@joinin.club You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children's Privacy

Join in is not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at hello@joinin.club and we will delete it.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and access controls on our backend. No system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
